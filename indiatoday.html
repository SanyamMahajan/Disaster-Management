Massive Earthquake Hits Northern India

A powerful earthquake with a magnitude of 7.4 struck northern India early on Tuesday, causing widespread panic and significant damage in several cities. The earthquake's epicenter was located near the Himalayan region, but tremors were felt as far as Delhi and surrounding areas.

Authorities reported that buildings in several districts have collapsed, and rescue teams are currently working to reach trapped individuals. So far, there have been reports of casualties, and the numbers are expected to rise as the rescue operations continue.

Prime Minister has called for an emergency meeting to assess the situation and coordinate relief efforts. In a statement, he urged citizens to stay calm and assured them that all necessary measures are being taken to ensure their safety and provide assistance to those affected.
