History of Indian Disasters

India has experienced numerous devastating disasters, both natural and man-made. Below is a table highlighting some of the significant disasters in Indian history:

Disaster Name Date Location Impact
2004 Indian Ocean Tsunami December 26, 2004 Coast of India Over 230,000 deaths across multiple countries
1999 Odisha Super Cyclone October 29, 1999 Odisha Approx. 10,000 deaths and widespread destruction
2013 Uttarakhand Floods June 16-17, 2013 Uttarakhand Over 5,000 deaths due to floods and landslides
Bhopal Gas Tragedy December 3, 1984 Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh Thousands killed and many affected by gas exposure
Kutch Earthquake January 26, 2001 Gujarat Over 20,000 deaths and massive destruction
2015 Chennai Floods November-December 2015 Chennai, Tamil Nadu Approx. 500 deaths and extensive flooding
Cyclone Fani May 3-4, 2019 Odisha 64 deaths and large-scale evacuations
2020 Assam Floods May-July 2020 Assam Approx. 128 deaths and widespread displacement
