Massive Flood Hits Northern India

A massive flood has displaced thousands of people in Northern India. NDTV has been covering the relief efforts, highlighting the role of local and national authorities in managing the situation. Volunteers and relief workers are providing essential services like food, water, and shelter to the affected population.

In the latest update, NDTV has reported that over 10,000 people have been evacuated and relief measures are scaling up. The Prime Minister has visited the area to assess the situation, and many are hoping for swift action in repairing the damage caused by this natural disaster.

Stay tuned to NDTV for live updates and exclusive interviews with those on the front lines of the relief efforts.
